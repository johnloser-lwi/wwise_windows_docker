Voice Pitch

The playback speed of an audio object, where:

  • Pitch 0 = Normal speed.
  • Pitch 1,200 = 2 x speed.
  • Pitch 2,400 = 4 x speed.
  • Pitch -1,200 = 0.5 speed
  • Pitch -2,400 = 0.25 speed

Tip: 1,200 cents is equivalent to one octave.

Default value: 0
Range: -2400 to 2400
Units: Cents
