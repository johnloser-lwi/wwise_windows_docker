Mix Behavior

The following possibilities are provided:

  • Use Default: Allows Wwise to choose the most suitable destination for this Audio Object. Refer to Understanding Object-Based Audio for further details.
  • Mix to Main: Forces the Audio Object to be routed to the Main Mix of the Audio Device.
  • Mix to Passthrough: Forces the Audio Object to be routed to the Passthrough Mix of the Audio Device. If Passthrough is not supported by the Audio Device, the Audio Object will be routed to the Main Mix.

Default value: Use Default
