Roll-off Factor

Specifies the slope of the attenuation curve, where higher values produce steeper slopes or faster attenuation.

At a value of 1, a -6 dB attenuation is applied each time the object’s distance is doubled after the Minimum distance. A roll-off of 2 creates a steeper slope that attenuates the woosh sound twice as fast. A roll-off of 0.5, on the other hand, creates a flatter slope that attenuates the woosh sound two times slower.

For more information about the attenuation model used by SoundSeed Wind, refer to Wwise SoundSeed Air - Woosh.

Default value: 1
Range: 0.1 to 4
