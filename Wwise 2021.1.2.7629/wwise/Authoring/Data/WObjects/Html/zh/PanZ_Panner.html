Pan Up-Down

The coordinate along the Z axis of the circle. （对高度分层中不含声道的配置没有影响）。

备注:The Z axis slider is only available when the Speaker Panning mode is set to Steering.

备注:The Z axis is not represented in the graph view; its value is controlled and indicated solely by the Z axis slider.

Default value: 0
Range: -100 to 100
