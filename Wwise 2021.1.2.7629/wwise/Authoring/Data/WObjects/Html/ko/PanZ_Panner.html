Pan Up-Down

The coordinate along the Z axis of the circle. (높이 레이어에 채널이 없는 구성에서는 아무 영향을 주지 않음.)

참고: The Z axis slider is only available when the Speaker Panning mode is set to Steering.

참고: The Z axis is not represented in the graph view; its value is controlled and indicated solely by the Z axis slider.

Default value: 0
Range: -100 to 100
