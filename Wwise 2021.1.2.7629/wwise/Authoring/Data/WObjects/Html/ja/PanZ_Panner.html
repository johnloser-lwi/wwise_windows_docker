Pan Up-Down

The coordinate along the Z axis of the circle. （コンフィグレーションにHeightレイヤのチャンネルがければ、影響はない。）

注釈:The Z axis slider is only available when the Speaker Panning mode is set to Steering.

注釈:The Z axis is not represented in the graph view; its value is controlled and indicated solely by the Z axis slider.

Default value: 0
Range: -100 to 100
